Monkey agreed and sat on the crocodile’s back so that the crocodile could take him to his house.
In the middle of the river, the crocodile began sinking in.
The monkey got frightened and asked the crocodile why he was sinking. 
Knowing that there was no escape for the monkey, the crocodile replied, 
“Forgive me, my friend. My wife needs to eat your heart to save her life".
